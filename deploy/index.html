ABOUT US

Cape May County Park Central is over 200 acres including the Zoo, with many forested areas maintained in their natural state. Within the park, you will find picnic areas, walking paths, bike paths, fishing ponds, disco golf course, playgrounds, and more!

Admission to the Park and Zoo is completely free as well as parking for cars. Usage of the picnic pavilions, the wedding gazebo, and groups visiting by bus or passenger vans are required to pay a fee and make a reservation.

ANIMAL ENCOUNTERS

Giraffe Encounter

Giraffe encounters occur daily at 11:30 am, 1:30 pm and 2:00 pm. During this approximately 20-minute encounter you will have the opportunity to stand on ground level with the giraffes and offer them 'browse' to eat, while our educators teach you all about the resident 'tower' of giraffes that call the Cape May County Zoo home!

Camel Encounter

Camel encounters are offered daily at 2:00 PM. During this approximately 30-minute encounter you will go behind the scenes with our Zoo Educators and learn about the amazing adaptions camels have to survive. You will have the opportunity to get up-close with both our Bactrian and Dromedary camels and offer them "browse".

Reptile Encounter

Reptile encounters are offered daily at 11:00 AM, but are weather dependent. During this approximately 30-minute encounter you will get to go behind the scenes with our Zoo Educators and meet our some of our cold-blooded residents. You will have the opportunity to explore our reptile building and offer some tasty treats to our some of our oldest residents as well as "Ike" the American Alligator!
