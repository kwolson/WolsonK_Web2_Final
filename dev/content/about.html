ABOUT US

Cape May County Park Central is over 200 acres including the Zoo, with many forested areas maintained in their natural state. Within the park, you will find picnic areas, walking paths, bike paths, fishing ponds, disco golf course, playgrounds, and more!

Admission to the Park and Zoo is completely free as well as parking for cars. Usage of the picnic pavilions, the wedding gazebo, and groups visiting by bus or passenger vans are required to pay a fee and make a reservation.
